Hi! I’m a student, researcher, software engineer, dancer, and foodie. I’m based in NYC.

I am interested in programming language (PL) and software engineering (SE) research, with an angle of human-computer interaction (HCI).

I am currently working as a research assistant on Fuzzy Mental Model model checker with Prof. Eunsuk Kang and Prof. Matthew Bolton, and secure code generation with LLMs with Prof. Baishakhi Ray and Prof. Junfeng Yang.

Most of my previous work was done as a research assistant with Prof. Mark Santolucito in Barnard Programming Languages Lab. Learn more in my publications page and projects page!
